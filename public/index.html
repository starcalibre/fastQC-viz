About

FastQC-viz: a web app for assessing the quality of sequencing data.

Quality control in sequencing experiments is vital for reducing the effects of technical biases in downstream analyses. FastQC-viz extends the output of FastQC to provide useful visualisations to check the quality of multiple samples obtained from an experiment.

Usage

List the text file output from each sample obtained from the FastQC analysis and click on a module of interest.
